Get to know Leanne and the Blissful Sleep Solutions story

Meet Leanne

I'm Leanne owner of Blissful Sleep Solutions and a busy mum of two beautiful children; my gorgeous, energetic and clever son Hamish and my cheeky, happy and adventurous daredevil daughter Hayley, there is never a dull moment. With a passion for helping people and teaching skills that I have mastered, I have embarked on this wonderful journey to help other families get the blissful sleep that they all deserve.

I have always had a passion for people, and for helping others in any way that I can. This, coupled with my enthusiasm for education and learning, lead me to obtain new skills so I could help others. With a degree in education and a natural skill for problem-solving, I entered the hospitality industry for 12 years at the management level, training new staff while implementing new procedures and providing great customer service. From there, I wanted to explore my options and change my career. I became a customer service officer for a large financial provider, opening the door to more self-learning and discovery. Throughout these roles, I've grown a lot personally and developed an enormous amount of service skills.

After having my son, going back to the office wasn't going to work for me. He was also a terrible sleeper and at 10 months, I began researching and looking for help. This is when I decided to make another career change and began my studies to become a certified infant and child sleep consultant. This was the best decision I ever made; my son went from waking every night to sleeping through with the help of another trainee sleep consultant. Throughout this journey, I've gained so much scientific and evidence-backed information about sleep that I'm now able to use to help other families solve their sleep problems.
